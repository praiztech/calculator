Keyboard Shortcuts

You can activate calculator buttons by pressing alt and the corresponding key on the keyboard. For example, press alt + 1 on the keyboard to activate 1 in the calculator.

Note! To activate Delete calculator button, press alt + backspace or alt + delete. To activate Reset calculator button, press alt + R or alt + C or alt + clear.
