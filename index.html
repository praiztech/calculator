Keyboard Shortcuts

You can enable keyboard shortcuts by activating the "Enable Shortcuts" checkbox and moving focus to the first button in the keypad. This allows you to activate keypad buttons by pressing the corresponding key on the keyboard. For example, pressing 1 on the keyboard activates 1 in the calculator when keyboard shortcuts is enabled.

Note! To activate the calculator's Delete button, press backspace or delete. To activate the calculator's Reset button, press shift + backspace or shift + delete.
